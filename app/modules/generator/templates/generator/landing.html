Generate feature models

Pick how you want to build your feature models. Both generators produce UVL files ready to download or upload to the hub.

Random generator

Available

Generate random feature models from parameters: feature count, tree depth, constraint mix, attribute catalog and probabilities. Runs entirely in your browser with Pyodide.

  • Boolean, arithmetic and type level toggles
  • Configurable constraints and attributes
  • Exports a ZIP of UVL files
Start

LLM generator

Experimental

Design feature models from a concept, or derive variants of an existing UVL from the hub. An open-weights LLM runs entirely in your browser (WebLLM) and flamapy validates every candidate. Nothing leaves your machine.

  • From concept: type a domain (e.g. "coffee shop"), get a valid UVL back
  • From seed: pick any UVL from UVLHub and get semantic variants of it
  • Optional cross-tree constraints, drop-on-fail validation
  • In-browser inference (WebGPU), no API key, nothing uploaded
